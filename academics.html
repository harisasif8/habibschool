Academics


Academic Session

The Academic session commences on 01st August and ends on 31st May of the next calendar year.


Evaluation

Strictly adhering to a pragmatic and balanced system students are evaluated once into month and at the end of each academic year. Records of teaching learning activities are maintained to ensure that the curriculum objectives are achieved. 1st bimonthly result + half yearly result + 2nd bimonthly result = Term Order (Term Average) (Average of Term Order (Term Average) + Annual Order (Annual Exam Result) / 2 = Final Order
