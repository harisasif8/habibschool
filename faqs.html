A: Registration forms are available from the school admission office when admissions are open.

Q: How will I know about the Dates?

A: Refer to the ‘Dates to Remember’ panel on this website under Admission process.

Q: What is the Fee structure for the session 2020-21?

A: Monthly Fee is around 12,000/- and Admission fee is 45,000/-

Q: Who is the Principal of HPS?

A: Mrs. Ainee Shehzad

Q: Who is the Examination Department Head of HPS?

A: Mr. Saleem Raza

A: Please contact Mr. Saleem Raza / Mr. Faham (Ext – 217)

Q: What are the School Timings of HPS?

A: Junior Section (07:55 am to 01:30 pm) and Senior Section (07:55 am to 03:30 pm)

A: Admission Process is completely fair, transparent and on merit basis hence no pressure is helpful.
