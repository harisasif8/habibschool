School Facilities


HPS emphasize a lot on the Sports hence there is a dedicated Sports Department with professional staff.


Sports & Physical Education


The sports department has always enjoyed an enviable position in Hockey, Swimming, and Athletics. The School has compulsory games period with facilities for outdoor activities under the guidance of experienced coaches. Many national icons in sports and renowned citizens have been nurtured in the school. These sports activities include Hockey, Swimming, Athletics, Basketball, Cricket, Gymnastics, Table Tennis, Tae Kwon Do and Volleyball. The spacious and well-kept grounds of the school provide avenues for sports and co-curricular activities, which form an integral part of academic life of students. The school has two hockey fields that have produced national players like, Sohail Abbas, Hasan Sardar,Qamar Ibrahim, Kamran Ashraf, Ahmed Alam, Safdar Abbas, and many more. The other feats of the school hockey team include winning the Pakistan Independence Day Hockey Tournament in 1999, The Fair Play Trophy Award in Muscat, Oman in 2001 and International Hockey Tournament for under 12 in Ipoh, Malaysia in 2003.
